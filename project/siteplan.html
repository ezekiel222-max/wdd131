Afrobeat Vibes

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Name: Afrobeat Vibes

Logo: A simple logo with a music note and African pattern style (to be designed later).

Why this name? The name "Afrobeat Vibes" captures the energetic and culturally rich spirit of Nigerian music and aims to appeal to fans of Afrobeat and related genres.

Optional Domain: afrobeatvibes.ng or afrobeat-vibes.com

Site Purpose

This site is dedicated to showcasing Nigerian music, especially Afrobeat culture. It provides fans with trending tracks, artist highlights, and interactive music content. Visitors can discover new music, learn about popular artists, and share feedback on their favorites.

Scenarios

  • Which Nigerian artists are currently trending?
  • Where can I listen to or find the most popular Afrobeat tracks this week?
  • How can I submit my favorite artist or song for a fan poll?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #1a1a1a (black) – used for background and headers
  • Accent Color: #ffcc00 (gold/yellow) – used for highlights, buttons, and links
  • Text Color: #ffffff (white) – used for body text on dark backgrounds

Typography

  • Heading Font: 'Oswald', sans-serif – for titles and section headings
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – for paragraphs and general content
